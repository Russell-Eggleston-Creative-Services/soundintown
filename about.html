About Us

Our mission is to help you look and sound great.  Located in Northern BC, Canada from Fort St. John, BC and serving the surrounding area.

Audio Visual Services

We provide audio visual services for event large and small.  We serve government, corporate, business, and the events market in Fort St. John, Dawson Creek, Chetwynd, Tumbler Ridge, Fort Nelson, Northern BC and Northern Alberta.

Company History

Sound in Town has been serving the area for over 15 years.  We started in live sound, providing sound services for events such as weddings and bars.  We have since grown into providing full prodouction services for events, audio visual intregrations, installations, and other services based around the local needs in our area for audio visual services. 

About SOUND IN TOWN

We help businesses, organizations, and government with audio, visual, and broadcasting needs for risk adverse events.  Our job is to make your event go smoothly and to make you look and sound great.
